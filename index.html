Album Description:

Imprint for Charity is an electronic music compilation album organized by Kiana Li, Adrian Wrobel & Oliver Varga. The latter two were involved in previous various artist projects, of which this compilation is a spiritual successor. Sourcing musical and graphic design talent from their corner of the electronic music scene, a roster was developed with an emphasis on properly representing LGBTQ voices. We aimed to curate an understated and no-frills collection of underground electronic sounds, ranging from ambient to dark drum & bass.

The charity compilation is in support of Mermaids, a UK charity and advocacy organization that supports gender-diverse and transgender youth. All proceeds from the album will be directed to Mermaids. From the team at Imprint, we hope you will support the album & our cause.
